Visuals
Two stage assistants bring in the tsukurimono and place it on Square 4. It is a kind of dais decorated with ropes and paper offerings, and represents Munechika's forge. Shortly after, the curtain lifts and the swordsmith enters in silence. He walks onto the stage, stops for a moment on Square 2 facing the audience, turns slightly toward the dais as if acknowledging its presence, and then turns and walks to Square 8. There, he kneels down facing diagonally away from the audience, becoming invisible, awaiting the beginning of the second act.

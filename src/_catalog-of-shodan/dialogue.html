Catalog of Shōdan: Spoken

Dialogue

Voice
Unmetered

A prose conversation between two characters delivered in kotoba. In style, it is similar to a Mondō, but their narratives differ. Often, the Mondō provides some information about the shite’s past and/or identity. The narrative of a Dialogue is more mundane. In Kokaji, it simply concludes the Waki Enters dan without adding new information.

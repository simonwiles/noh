Mid exit

The First Act exit of a shite personifying a deity, could be accompanied by an exit music, such as a Raijo . This is the case in Kokaji. In Hashitomi, the shite personifies a humble young lady, thus, it is in silence that she leaves the stage. The slow yet balanced steps fill the silence with air of dignity.

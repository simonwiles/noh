Two stage assistants remove the stage props, before the performers leave the stage.

The exit of the actors and musicians happens in silence. The shite is the first one to exit, followed by the waki. If a tsukurimono was used in the play, the stage assistants would remove it next. Once the actor or stage assistant has passed the Second Pine, the curtain rises and then falls once he has entered the anteroom. From this point, the musicians exit in the same order they entered, the nokhan player first, followed by the kotsuzumi, ≈çtsuzumi,and taiko players. Upon reaching the Second Pine, the curtain is pulled back on one side to allow for the musicians' exit. The jiutai members exit through the back left side door, starting with the back row members. As they are exiting the stage, actors, assistants and musicians exhibit modesty and decorum. The audience holds its applause until the last musician is about to exit.

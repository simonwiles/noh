Form-Appendix

A model for the two-act plays showing shōdan commonly associated with particular dan.

ACT 1
Waki Enters (with tsure if present) Entrance music: Shidai , Issei , Nanoribue
Entrance chant: Shidai , Issei , but skipped if Nanoribue was played as entrance music.
Spoken self-Introduction: Nanori
Travel chant: Michiyuki
Arrival at destination annoucement: Tsukizerifu
Shite Enters (with tsure if any) Entrance music Shidai , Issei
Entrance chant: Shidai , Issei
Tri-part descriptive or lyrical passage: Sashi, Sageuta, Ageuta
Dialogue Dialogue Mondō
Narrative: Kakeai
Preliminary recitative: Kakaru
Recitative: Sashi
Reading of a letter: Fumi
Lament: Kudoki
Shite Performs Kusemai Shidai-chant, Issei-chant, a short dance such as an Iroe mai, Kuri, Sashi, Kuse (dance to text)
Shite Exits Discussion: Mondō or Rongi
Exit music: Raijo , Haya-tsuzumi, ...
ACT 2
Waki Waits Waiting chant: Machiutai
Shite Re-enters Entrance music: Issei , Hayafue, Deha, Sagariha, or Obeshi
Entrance chant: Issei
Dialogue Mondō, Rongi
Shite Performs Instrumental dance: Jonomai, Maibataraki , Gaku, Hataraki-goto...
Shite Exits Kiri (dance to text)
